What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is the power obtained by harnessing the energy of the sun’s rays. This energy is renewable, meaning it is a sustainable source of energy that does not deplete over time. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, which can be used to power homes, businesses, and devices.

Benefits of Solar Energy

Solar energy offers numerous benefits, including reduced electricity bills, lower carbon footprint, and energy independence. It’s environmentally friendly, produces no pollution, and is one of the cleanest sources of energy available.

Growth of Solar Energy

The solar energy industry has seen rapid growth in recent years. Thanks to advancements in technology and the push for green energy, solar power has become more affordable and accessible. Countries worldwide are investing in solar infrastructure to reduce dependency on fossil fuels.
